# Naming Conventions

## Files
- snake_case → user_profile_screen.dart
- suffix-based naming:
  - _screen.dart
  - _widget.dart
  - _provider.dart
  - _service.dart

## Classes
- PascalCase → UserProfileScreen

## Variables
- camelCase → userName

## Constants
- camelCase or kPrefix → kPrimaryColor

## Folders
- feature-based naming → auth/, home/, chat/
